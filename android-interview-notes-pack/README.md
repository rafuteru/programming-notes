## Notes about accuracy
These notes are intentionally conservative:
- Claims are aligned to official Android or Kotlin documentation where possible.
- Repository-specific third-party links were not treated as source of truth unless they point back to official docs.
- Some topics in interviews are opinionated; those are labeled as “recommended answer” rather than “absolute rule”.
